Chess Game

Game Overview:

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players. It's known for its complexity and depth, requiring skillful planning and tactical thinking.

How to Play:

  • Each player controls an army of pieces including pawns, rooks, knights, bishops, queens, and a king.
  • The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, which means the king is under attack and cannot escape capture.
  • Players take turns to make moves according to specific rules for each piece.

Enjoy the intellectual challenge of this timeless classic!

Development and Legacy:

Chess has a long history dating back to ancient times and has evolved into its modern form over centuries. It remains a cornerstone of strategic games and is widely studied and played worldwide.
