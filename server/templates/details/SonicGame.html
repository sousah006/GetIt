Sonic-Game

"Sonic-Game is an inspired version of the classic platform game starring Sonic the Hedgehog. Developed by a passionate fan, the game captures the essence of speed and adventure that defines the series."

Features:

  • Fast-paced gameplay with Sonic's characteristic speed.
  • Diverse levels with challenging obstacles and enemies.
  • Collect rings to gain points and extra lives.

History:

Sonic the Hedgehog was created by Sega in 1991 as a response to Nintendo's Mario. It became a flagship franchise and a symbol of the Sega Genesis console's success, captivating gamers worldwide with its vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay.
